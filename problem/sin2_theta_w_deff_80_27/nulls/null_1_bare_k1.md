    gap = (0.23121 − 0.22857) = +0.00264   (1.14% relative)
    z   = 0.00264 / 0.00004 = 66 σ

**Far outside Z1 (≤ 1σ).**

## Status

`sin²θ_W = 8/35` as a direct M_Z prediction is **Class 1 numerology**
per `../../../sync_cost/derivations/numerology_inventory.md:37–58`
and `../../../MANIFEST.yml:bare_k1_identities.sin2_theta_W`.

The bare identity `8/35` remains true at K=1 as a number-theoretic
fact. Its use as a prediction at any physical scale is declined.

## What this null tells the current problem

The proposal `sin²θ_W = q₂^{d_eff} / (q₂^{d_eff} + q₃^{d_eff})` with
`d_eff ≠ d` must move the value from 0.22857 (d=3) to something
in Z1-range of 0.23121. The d_eff formula with `d_eff = 80/27`
delivers 0.23123 (0.5σ) — but that's numerology until `gaps/g1`
derives the substitution structurally.
